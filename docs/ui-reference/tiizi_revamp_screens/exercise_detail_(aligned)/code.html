Pushups

Strength
auto_awesome

Benefits

Builds upper body strength and core stability.

Metric Unit

REPS

Recommended

10-20 per set

menu_book Instructions

settings_accessibility Setup

Start in a high plank position. Place your hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart, with your feet together or slightly apart. Ensure your body forms a straight line from head to heels.

play_circle Execution

Lower your body until your chest nearly touches the floor. Pause briefly at the bottom, then push yourself back up to the starting position by extending your arms fully.

tips_and_updates Cues

  • check_circle Elbows at 45 degrees
